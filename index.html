About FOB Beats


Fionn O'Brien aka F.O.B is an established Music Producer, Beat Maker and Mixing/Mastering Engineer from the Republic of Ireland. Having graduated from Dundalk Institute of Technology with a First Class Honours degree in the Production of Music and Audio, F.O.B has accumulated over 1,000,000 Spotify streams, most notably for his work with Irish singer Matthew Nolan and Hip Hop artist Uppbeat.
